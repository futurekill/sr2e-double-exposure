# Changelog

## 0.1.0 (in development)

First content drop — the module is one-click importable and the two opening
runs are playable.

### Module
- Scaffolded the `sr2e-double-exposure` module: `module.json` requiring the
  `sr2e` system (≥ 0.9.0), four packs (Adventure + Scenes/Cast/Journals), and
  pack-build tooling (with JournalEntry page-splitting).

### Cast (`de-actors`)
- 8 NPCs as SR2E `npc` actors with skills, armour, initiative, and GM bios,
  transcribed from the page renders: Jonathan Tung, the Hive Queen (Force-10
  boss), Special Agent Simon Juárez, true-form soldier & worker insect spirits,
  a DocWagon courier, and the Aztechnology security guard and rigger-driver.

### Scenes (`de-scenes`)
- 6 placeholder maps with labeled grid backgrounds for the major locations
  (Hope Relief Camp compound & registration, the Hive, New Dawn research lab,
  the cyber-shipment route, the meet) — swap each background for final art.

### Journals (`de-journals`)
- GM Overview (Premise, Plot Synopsis, How It Runs, Key Cast).
- Encounter 1 — *Digging Their Own Graves* (the Aztech convoy warm-up).
- Encounter 2 — *Six Feet Under* (the DocWagon DNA-case warm-up).

### Adventure (`double-exposure`)
- One-click Adventure document bundling all of the above for world import.

### Remaining for 0.1.0
- The main Project Hope investigation encounters + the "Here Comes the Queen"
  climax; the custom tables (Awareness Points, Manual Detection) and the player
  handout; final map art.
